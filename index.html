Singapore Payment Methods


Explore how people pay in Singapore for transport, shopping, etc.

EZ-Link

Contactless stored-value cards used mainly for public transport fares and small retail payments.

Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)

Widely accepted contactless payment cards used for retail and public transport via SimplyGo.

Mobile Wallets

Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay use NFC technology linked to your bank cards for easy contactless payments.

PayNow

Real-time funds transfer using phone numbers, NRIC, or QR codes. Popular for peer-to-peer and retail payments.

FavePay

A widely accepted QR payment system in restaurants, bakeries and retail shops.

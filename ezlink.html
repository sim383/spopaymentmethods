EZ-Link Card

The EZ-Link card is a widely used stored-value contactless smart card in Singapore, mainly for public transport fares and retail payments at participating merchants. It works by tapping the card on card readers in buses, MRT stations, and other locations.

It is not linked to your bank account and requires you to top up the card balance regularly at top-up machines, convenience stores, or via mobile apps.




How to Use EZ-Link Card

  1. Tap In: When boarding a bus or entering an MRT station, tap your EZ-Link card on the card reader to deduct the fare.

  2. Tap Out: Tap your card again when exiting the bus or MRT station to complete the trip and deduct the correct fare.

  3. Check Balance: You can check your card balance at top-up machines, retail outlets, or via mobile apps linked to your EZ-Link card.

  4. Top Up: Add value to your EZ-Link card using cash or card at top-up points or using the EZ-Link app.

  5. Use for Retail: You can also use your EZ-Link card for small payments at stores, vending machines, and food courts that accept EZ-Link payments.

Credit / Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)

Credit and debit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express are widely accepted in Singapore at most retail stores, restaurants, and service providers. Many payment terminals now support contactless payments, making transactions fast and convenient.

You can also link your credit or debit card to SimplyGo to pay for public transport fares directly without needing a separate card.



How to Use Your Card for Payments

  1. Check the payment terminal: Ensure it supports contactless payments or insert/swipe your card if needed.

  2. Tap or insert your card: For contactless, simply tap your card near the terminal's NFC reader. Otherwise, insert your card into the chip reader or swipe.

  3. Confirm the amount: Check the transaction amount displayed on the terminal screen.

  4. Authorize the payment: For contactless payments under a certain limit, no PIN may be needed. For higher amounts, enter your PIN or sign as required.

  5. Wait for confirmation: The terminal will show a confirmation message and you may hear a beep or see a green light indicating payment success.

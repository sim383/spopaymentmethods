PayNow

PayNow is a fast and secure peer-to-peer funds transfer service in Singapore, linking your bank account to your mobile number or NRIC/FIN for easy payments. It allows users to send or receive money instantly without needing to know the recipient’s bank account details as well as purchase items at shops.

PayNow is widely used for personal payments, small business transactions, and government payments.



How to Use PayNow

  1. Register: Link your mobile number or NRIC/FIN to your bank account via your bank’s mobile app or internet banking.

  2. Select PayNow: In your bank app, choose PayNow as the payment method.

  3. Scan QR Code: Scan the merchant's QR code in the shop.

  4. Enter Amount: Input the amount you want to transfer.

  5. Confirm Payment: Review the details and confirm the transaction. The funds are transferred instantly.

Mobile Wallets

Mobile wallets like Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Samsung Pay let users pay quickly and securely with their smartphones or smartwatches. These wallets store your credit or debit card information and use NFC (near-field communication) technology for contactless payments.

Many merchants and public transport in Singapore accept mobile wallet payments through SimplyGo, making it easy to pay without carrying physical cards.



How to Use Mobile Wallets for Payment

  1. Set up your mobile wallet: Add your credit or debit cards to Apple Pay, Google Pay, or Samsung Pay app on your device.

  2. Unlock your phone or watch: Use Face ID, fingerprint, or passcode to unlock your device before payment.

  3. Tap near the payment terminal: Hold your device close to the NFC reader until you see a confirmation.

  4. Wait for confirmation: The terminal and your device will confirm payment with a sound or vibration.

  5. Collect your receipt: Take the receipt if needed, and you're done!
